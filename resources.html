Resources for animal welfare

Grants, emergency vet assistance, food programs, and support for families, rescuers, and shelters. We keep this list so no one has to search alone. We are adding links as we verify them, so check back often.

Foster support
Foster pet stipends
Many rescues offer stipends for food, supplies, and vet care. Ask before you assume you have to cover it all.
Temporary foster programs
Short term care for pets of families facing domestic violence, homelessness, or hospitalization.
Community based support
Humane society food pantries
Many local humane societies run free pet food pantries. Call yours and ask.
Community pet food banks
Volunteer run food distribution for families in need.

Delivers pet food to seniors along with their own meals.

Funding for trap, neuter, and return programs that care for community cats.

Local volunteer networks for temporary fostering, transport, and supplies.
